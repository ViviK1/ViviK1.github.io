Chiang Rai

If you like a peaceful and non-rushing lifestyle, Chiang Rai is undeniably a place for you! Though sometimes traffic here can be as crazy as in hectic Chiang Mai (mainly in the morning hours), don't be pulled back. A small number of foreigners, very friendly, humble and easy-going locals (primarily non-English speaking) will compensate for it.
